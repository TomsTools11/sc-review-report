Bid adjustments — Auto (0 of 10 from 4/29 plan executed)

High priority

1. Pull the four 0%-contact-rate sources hard

Premium PS 5: 244% → 75% Premium Email B: 249% → 75% Paid Search: 100% → 75% Native/Display/Social: 48% → 30%

These four sources combined for ~$13 in spend with zero contacts over 60 days. Native/Display alone holds 45,243 opportunities — pulling it to 30% caps exposure without removing the source.

High priority

2. Push the two call-driven winners — Premium PS 2B and Premium PS 6

Premium PS 2B: 197% → 200% Premium PS 6: 164% → 200%

Both running 200% contact rate (more contacts than clicks via inbound calls). Both already above 1st position market price — the additional push is to dominate the auction and pull more of the existing opportunity flow into impressions.

Medium priority

3. Open Email and Premium Referral — they're the largest dark sources

Email: 51% → 85% Premium Referral: 86% → 110%

Email holds 5,147 opportunities at 6.1% bid rate; Premium Referral 5,052 at 6.6%. Both have shown strong call patterns on the Home side. Worth a controlled test at market-band pricing.

Medium priority

4. Trim Premium PS 4 — paying way above 1st but no yield

Premium PS 4: 301% → 200%

Effective bid is $17.61 against a 1st-position RPC of $14.21. Spending above market without contact returns. Pull to 200% keeps participation but stops the overpay.
