Under a normalized schedule, Tier 3 resets 120→95% ($3.94 ≈ its current placed bid, ~$3.85) so its level holds approximately flat. The bid-rate review (3.0% of a 6,605-opportunity pool; Tier 1 / Tier 3 overlap) continues. No raise.
4
Raise Tier 2 and Home SearchB with the bundle — pending approval
Tier 2 70→90% ($3.74 ≈ 2nd position, $3.64) and Home SearchB 155→165% ($6.85 ≈ 2nd position, $6.83). Both raises apply only if the schedule change ships; without it, placed bids stay below the target positions.

Scope the Mobile Home sub-campaign
