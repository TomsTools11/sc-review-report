ICP — non-standard, unchanged

Supreme Choice serves a non-standard auto market that competing agencies typically avoid: consumers with prior accidents, DUIs, lapsed coverage, or who currently have no insurance. The campaign's "Currently Insured = No" filter is intentional and stays in place. We do not target standard insured comparison shoppers — that's not the book Supreme Choice is trying to grow.

The bid moves above are aimed exclusively at winning more of the right kind of leads. By concentrating budget on the 6 sources that historically produced policies for this audience, we keep the funnel tight and the lead-to-sale CVR strong (~3% at peak — well above the 2% benchmark). The SR-22 / DUI positive-modifier test is queued for after Premium Referral 2 routing is fixed and Auto volume returns.
