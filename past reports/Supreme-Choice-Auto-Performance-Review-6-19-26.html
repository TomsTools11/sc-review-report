Over the last 30 days (through June 18, 2026), CA Auto delivered 49 leads and 4 inbound calls from 5,393 in-market searches, at $472.60 in ad spend — a 1.35% win rate at average position 11.8. The base bid holds at $2.86 — the work now is efficiency: trim two over-market paid-search sources, resolve the one 0%-win structural source, and hold the producers.
