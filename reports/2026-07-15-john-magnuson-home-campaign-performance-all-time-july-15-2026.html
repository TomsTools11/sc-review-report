06

Recommendations

Cycle beginning July 15, 2026

1
Route inbound calls to a dedicated line
16 inbound calls are recorded campaign to date. Inbound calls reach a prospect who has tapped to call directly from the ad. Routing them to a number separate from the telemarketing service, then to the first available producer, is the change with the shortest path to a quote. We are sending call-routing options used by comparable agencies.
2
Keep contact intensity on the 127 uncontacted leads
84 of 211 leads are contacted (39.8%) and 12 quoted (5.7%). Quote-of-contacted is 14.3%. The full 30-day cadence by channel and week is in the follow-up guide:
Follow-up best practices
30-day cadence · phone, text, and email
Open guide →
3
Hold source bids until a current RightPricing report is pulled
The KS Home RightPricing file on record was pulled April 20, 2026, and reports a 1st-position rate below the 2nd-position rate on Tier 1 and Tier 3. Tier 2 runs at position 1.51 on 45.7% of budget for 37.4% of leads, while Tier 3 delivers 29.4% of leads on 16.3% of budget at a 46.8% contact rate. We are pulling a current report before changing any bid.
4
Hold targeting on age and property age
79.1% of leads are 65 or older and 42.2% sit on properties 30–39 years old. The 80+ band carries 5 of 12 quotes and the 30–39 year band carries 8 of 12. At 12 quotes campaign-wide, these splits are directional only. We are holding targeting and re-reading both dimensions once quote volume supports them.
5
Send bind data before each review
No policies are recorded as bound campaign to date. Bind data exported from the CRM lets us calculate cost per policy by source, age band, and geography, and direct budget toward what closes.
