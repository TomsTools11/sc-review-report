Hold Tier 2 at 70% as the cost anchor
Tier 2 carries the most leads (88) at the cheapest CPC ($2.04). Hold it; headroom exists if more volume is wanted later.

Investigate the Tier 3 bid rate
Tier 3 wins 41% at position 1.9 but bids on only 2.5% of its pool — a likely Tier 1 / Tier 3 geographic overlap. Hold the multiplier and confirm a single ad group per campaign; do not raise.
