The 4/17 peak-era playbook is the right destination.

Aug–Oct 2025's pattern — defended Tier 2, controlled Home SearchB push, off-hours bid kick, call-CTA creative, currently-insured=no filter — is replicable. The 5/4 plan is the first half of that restoration. Remaining items: Tier 1 publisher audit, off-hours bid kick restored, Home call-CTA creative rotation, SR-22 as a positive modifier (Auto first). Keep the path; the trend agrees.
